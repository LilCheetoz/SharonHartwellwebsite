About Sharon

Serving Bulkley Valley-Stikine

Sharon Hartwell was elected as the MLA for Bulkley Valley-Stikine in the 2024 provincial election, becoming the first Conservative MLA to represent this riding in a generation. She serves as the Official Opposition Critic for Rural Communities and Rural Development.

Raised in northern BC, Sharon understands the unique challenges and opportunities facing rural communities. Her work in Victoria is driven by a commitment to ensuring every corner of the province receives the attention and investment it deserves.

The Riding

Bulkley Valley-Stikine

Covering a vast area of northwestern BC, the Bulkley Valley-Stikine riding encompasses a diverse landscape of mountains, rivers, and thriving communities. From Smithers to Burns Lake to Telegraph Creek, this is one of BC's most spectacular regions.

With communities depending on resource industries, agriculture, and small business, the riding's residents deserve strong representation and a voice in decisions that affect their daily lives.
