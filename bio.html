Her Story

A Lifelong Commitment to Northern BC

Sharon Hartwell was elected as the Member of the Legislative Assembly for Bulkley Valley-Stikine in the 2024 provincial election, bringing with her a deep understanding of the challenges and opportunities facing rural and northern British Columbia.

Having spent her life in the communities of this region, Sharon brings a genuine, grounded perspective to the legislature. She understands that the needs of rural BC are distinct from those of the Lower Mainland — and that strong, consistent advocacy is needed to ensure northern communities receive their fair share of provincial investment and attention.

Background & Community Roots

Before entering politics, Sharon was deeply involved in her local community through [community involvement details to be added]. Her work brought her into contact with the everyday concerns of families, small business owners, seniors, and young people across the riding.

This experience shaped her belief that good government is about listening first — understanding the real needs of people before crafting solutions. It is an approach she has carried directly into her role at the Legislature.

In the Legislature

As the Official Opposition Critic for Rural Communities and Rural Development, Sharon ensures that the voices of northern and rural British Columbians are heard at every level of the legislative process. She participates actively in committees, debates, and question period to hold the government accountable on issues that matter to Bulkley Valley-Stikine residents.

Sharon is committed to working across party lines when it benefits her constituents, and to maintaining an open and accessible office so that every person in the riding knows they have a representative who will listen and act on their behalf.

Key Priorities

  • Improving healthcare access and infrastructure in rural BC
  • Supporting resource industries and the workers who depend on them
  • Ensuring adequate highway maintenance and rural transportation funding
  • Advocating for seniors' services and long-term care in the north
  • Protecting the natural environment while supporting sustainable development
  • Expanding internet and cellular connectivity for rural communities
  • Supporting First Nations partnerships and reconciliation

"The people of Bulkley Valley-Stikine deserve a representative who will fight for them every single day — someone who understands where they live and what they need. That is the commitment I bring to this role."

— Sharon Hartwell, MLA
