Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will my inquiry be responded to?

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 2–3 business days. For urgent matters — particularly those with government deadlines — please call the office directly so we can prioritize your case.

Can Sharon help me with a federal government issue?

As a provincial MLA, Sharon's primary role is with the BC Legislature. For federal issues (EI, CPP, immigration, passports), our office can refer you to the appropriate federal contacts and your Member of Parliament.

Do I need to be a Conservative supporter to get help from this office?

Absolutely not. Sharon's constituency office is here to serve all residents of Bulkley Valley-Stikine, regardless of their political views or party affiliation. Every constituent deserves support.

How do I request Sharon to speak at my event?

Please use the contact form above or call the office directly. Include details about your event (date, location, audience, topic) and we will do our best to accommodate your request based on availability.

Is there a toll-free number for constituents outside Smithers?

Yes, there is a toll-free number available for constituents calling from across the riding. Please contact the office directly to obtain the current toll-free number.
