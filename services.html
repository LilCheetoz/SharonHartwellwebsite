Constituency & General Assistance

Our constituency office helps residents with a wide range of issues related to provincial government services and everyday concerns. If you're not sure who to call, start here.

📝

General Correspondence

Writing letters to government ministries or agencies on your behalf, and following up on outstanding issues.

🏘️

Community Issues

Raising community concerns — from road conditions to local facility needs — with the relevant provincial authorities.

📞

Government Referrals

Directing you to the right ministry, department, or agency for your specific issue so you don't get lost in the system.

🏫

School & Education Issues

Helping families navigate school funding, special needs support, and concerns related to the K-12 education system.

🌾

Agricultural Support

Assisting farmers and ranchers with provincial programs, permit issues, and advocacy for the agricultural sector.

💼

Small Business Assistance

Connecting business owners with provincial programs, licensing information, and regulatory guidance.

Not Sure Where to Start?

Call or email the constituency office. Our staff will listen to your concern and direct you to the right resources or advocate on your behalf. Contact us →

Provincial Government Services

Sharon's office can help you access provincial programs and services, resolve issues with provincial ministries, and advocate for better services in our region.

🏥

Healthcare & MSP

Help with Medical Services Plan, wait times, finding a family doctor, and accessing Northern Health services.

🤲

Income Assistance

Navigating Ministry of Social Development programs, disability assistance, and income support applications.

🚗

ICBC & Driver Licensing

Assistance with ICBC claims, driver licensing issues, and related motor vehicle matters.

🌳

Natural Resources & Permits

Help with forestry permits, Crown land applications, environmental assessments, and resource licensing.

🏗️

Transportation & Roads

Reporting road maintenance issues, advocating for highway improvements, and connecting with the Ministry of Transportation.

👶

Children & Family Services

Assistance with Ministry of Children and Family Development programs, childcare subsidies, and family support.

🎓

Post-Secondary Education

Information about student loans, BC scholarships, and accessing trades training and post-secondary programs.

🏠

Housing

Connecting you with BC Housing programs, subsidized housing lists, and rental support programs.

Municipal & Regional Liaison

While municipal issues are primarily handled by local government, Sharon's office can help facilitate communication, share resources, and advocate for provincial support for local priorities.

🏙️

Municipal Government Liaison

Facilitating communication between local governments in the riding and the provincial government on shared priorities.

💰

Grant Information

Information about provincial and federal grants available to local governments, non-profits, and community organizations.

🚒

Emergency Services

Connecting communities with provincial resources for volunteer fire departments, emergency preparedness, and disaster response.

♻️

Regional District Issues

Helping residents understand regional district services and connecting local concerns with the appropriate provincial contacts.

🌊

Water & Infrastructure

Advocacy for provincial investment in rural water systems, sewage, and infrastructure in unincorporated areas.

🏟️

Community Facilities

Supporting communities seeking provincial funding for recreational facilities, community halls, and public spaces.

Local Government Contacts

For issues directly within municipal jurisdiction — bylaw, local taxation, municipal permits — please contact your local city, town, or district office directly. Sharon's office can help direct you to the right contact.

Federal Service Referrals

As a provincial MLA, Sharon's primary role is with the BC Legislature. However, her office can help direct you to the appropriate federal services and your federal Member of Parliament for matters under federal jurisdiction.

📘

Passports & Citizenship

For passport applications, citizenship ceremonies, and immigration inquiries, we can refer you to the appropriate federal offices and your MP.

👴

Canada Pension Plan & OAS

Directing seniors and others to Service Canada for CPP, Old Age Security, and GIS applications and inquiries.

💼

Employment Insurance

Connecting workers with Service Canada regarding EI applications, appeals, and related federal employment programs.

🌐

Immigration & Refugees

Referrals to your federal MP's office and IRCC for immigration, work permits, and refugee matters.

🎖️

Veterans Affairs

Directing veterans and their families to the appropriate Veterans Affairs Canada resources and supports.

🌾

Federal Agricultural Programs

Information and referrals for Agriculture and Agri-Food Canada programs available to farmers and producers in the riding.

Your Federal MP

For federal matters, your primary contact is your Member of Parliament. Sharon's office can help direct you to the right federal contact and, where appropriate, coordinate advocacy efforts on your behalf.

Ready to Get Help?

Contact Sharon's constituency office today. Our team is available Monday to Friday to assist you.
